Clients

We offer energy efficient solution to following listed clients to improve the value, reliability and efficiency of project.

Hospitality

Any building in which sleeping accommodation is provided for commercial purposes, except any building classified under Health Care.

Educational

Any building used for schools, colleges, universities, and other training institutions for day-care purposes involving assembly for instruction, education, or recreation for students.

Health Care

Any building or part thereof, which is used for purposes such as medical or other treatment or care of persons suffering from physical or mental illness.

Retail

Any building or part thereof, which is used as shops, stores, market, for display and sale of merchandise, either wholesale or retail.

Business

Any building or part thereof which is used for transaction of business, for keeping of accounts and records and similar purposes, professional establishments, and service facilities.

Factory

Any building or part thereof, which is used as parts assembly, machine station and workshop.
